Problem 1629 - 2022 DGO Team P2 of 6, G

Difficulty: 8

Let \(\omega\) and \(I\) be the incircle and incenter of an acute triangle \(ABC\). Let \(\omega\) meet \(BC\) at \(D\). A point \(P\) is moves on line \(ID\). \(W\) is the reflection of \(P\) across the circumcenter of triangle \(PBC\). Point \(F\) lies on line \(AW\) such that \(PF \parallel AI\). Prove that when \(P\) moves on \(ID\), \(F\) lies on a line that is independent of \(P\).
