Problem 1796 - 2024 MODS Geometry Contest, P2 of 6, G

Difficulty: 7

Let \(\triangle ABC\) have incenter \(I\) and let its incircle \(\omega\) touch \(CA\), \(AB\) at points \(E\), \(F\). Let the nine-point circle of \(\triangle BIC\) meet \(\omega\) at \(T \notin BC\) and let lines \(BI\), \(CI\) meet line \(EF\) at points \(X\), \(Y\).

Prove that an intersection point of circles \(TYE\), \(TXF\) lies on line \(AI\).
