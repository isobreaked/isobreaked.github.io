Problem 458 - 2018 Balkan MO, P2 of 4

Difficulty: 6

Let \(q\) be a positive rational number. Two ants are initially at the same point \(X\) in the plane. In the \(n\)-th minute \((n = 1,2,\dots)\) each of them chooses whether to walk due north, east, south or west and then walks the distance of \(q^n\) metres. After a whole number of minutes, they are at the same point in the plane (not necessarily \(X\)), but have not taken exactly the same route within that time. Determine all possible values of \(q\).
