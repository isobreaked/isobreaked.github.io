Problem 1077 - 2021 IMO Malaysian Training Camp 1, G

Difficulty: 8

Let \(ABC\) be an acute triangle with \(AB < AC\). Let \(\Gamma\) be its circumcircle, \(I\) its incenter and \(P\) a point on \(\Gamma\) such that \(\angle API=90^{\circ}\). Let \(Q\) be a point on arc \(BAC\) such that \[QB\cdot\tan \angle B=QC\cdot \tan \angle C.\] Consider a point \(R\) such that \(PR\) is tangent to \(\Gamma\) and \(BR=CR\). Prove that the points \(A, Q, R\) are collinear.
