Problem 2049 - 2020 LMAO, P1 of 6

Difficulty: 7

Let \(k\) be a fixed positive integer. Suppose a function \(f: \mathbf{Z} \rightarrow \mathbf{R}\) satisfies:

  • \(f(xy) \geq f(x)f(y)\) for all \(x,y \in \mathbf{Z}\)
  • \(f(x+k)=f(x)\) for all \(x \in \mathbf{Z}\)
Prove that\[\sum_{i=1}^{k} f(i) \geq 0\]
