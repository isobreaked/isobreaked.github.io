Problem 2167 - 2000 IMOSL, G6, G

Difficulty: 7

\(ABCD\) is a convex quadrilateral. The perpendicular bisectors of \(AB\) and \(CD\) meet at \(Y\). \(X\) is a point inside \(ABCD\) such that \(\angle ADX = \angle BCX < 90^{\circ}\) and \(\angle DAX = \angle CBX < 90^{\circ}\). Show that \(\angle AYB = 2\angle ADX\).
