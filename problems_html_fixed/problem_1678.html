Problem 1678 - 2023 MODSMO Round 2, P6 of 6, N

Difficulty: 9

Let \(p\) be a prime such that \(\frac{p-1}2\) is also prime. A pair of integers \((x,y)\) with \(1 \le x,y \le p-1\) is called a commuter if at least one of \(x^y-y^x\) or \(x^y+y^x\) is divisible by \(p\). Show that the number of commuters is at most \(4.2p\sqrt{p}\).
