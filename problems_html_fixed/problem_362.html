Problem 362 - 2007 USAMO, P3 of 6

Difficulty: 9

Let \(S\) be a set containing \(n^2 + n - 1\) elements for some positive integer \(n.\) Suppose that the \(n\)-element subsets of \(S\) are partitioned into two classes. Prove that there are \(n\) pairwise disjoint sets in the same class.
