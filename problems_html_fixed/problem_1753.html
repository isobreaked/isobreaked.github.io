Problem 1753 - 2007 IMOSL, G2, G

Difficulty: 6

Denote by \(M\) the midpoint of side \(BC\) in an isosceles triangle \(ABC\) with \(AC = AB\). Take a point \(X\) on a smaller arc \(MA\) of the circumcircle of triangle \(\triangle ABM\). Denote by \(T\) the point inside of angle \(BMA\) such that \(\angle TMX = 90\) and \(TX = BX\). Prove that \(\angle MTB - \angle CTM\) does not depend on the choice of \(X\).
