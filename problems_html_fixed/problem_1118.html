Problem 1118 - 2022 DAMO, P1 of 6, CG

Difficulty: 7

101 distinct rays lie on a plane. No two rays share an endpoint or coincide, and no three rays concur. Find the maximum number of unordered pairs \((a, b)\) of rays such that \(a\) and \(b\) intersect at a point \(C\) which is equidistant from the endpoints of \(a\) and \(b\).
