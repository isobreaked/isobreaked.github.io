Problem 1097 - 2021-2022 ToT Fall, Senior A P5 of 7, G

Difficulty: 8

Let \(ABCD\) be a parallelogram and let \(P\) be a point inside it such that \(\angle PDA=\angle PBA\). Let \(\omega_1\) be the excircle of \(PAB\) opposite to the vertex \(A\). Let \(\omega_2\) be the incircle of triangle \(PCD\). Prove that one of the common tangents of \(\omega_1, \omega_2\) is parallel to \(AD\).
