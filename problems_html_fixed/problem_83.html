Problem 83 - 2011 USAMO, P6 of 6

Difficulty: 8

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be a set with \(225\) elements. Suppose further that there are eleven subsets of \(\mathcal{A}\) such that each \(\mathcal{A}_i\) has \(45\) elements, and \(\vert \mathcal{A}_i \cap \mathcal{A}_j \vert = 9\) \(\forall\) \(1 \leq i < j \leq 11\). Prove that \(\vert \mathcal{A}_1 \cup \mathcal{A}_2 \cup\cdots \cup \mathcal{A}_{11} \vert \geq 165\), and give an example for which equality holds.
