Problem 1998 - 2017 ITAMO, P5 of 6, CN

Difficulty: 6

Let \(x_1 , x_2, x_3,\ldots\) be a sequence of positive integers such that for every couple of positive integers \((m,n)\) we have \[x_{mn} \neq x_{m(n+1)}\] Prove that there exists a positive integer \(i\) such that \(x_i \ge 2017\).

Idea

Construct unresolved case from resolved case cleverly is a recurring pattern.
