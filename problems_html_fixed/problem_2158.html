Problem 2158 - 2025 Polish MO, Round 1, P10 of 12, G

Difficulty: 6

Let \(ABC\) be an acute triangle with circumcircle \(\omega\) and \(AB < AC\). Let \(M\) and \(N\) be the midpoint of the longer arc \(BC\) and shorter arc \(BC\) of \(\omega\), respectively. Let \(X \neq M\) and \(Y \neq M\) be points on the line \(AM\) such that \(BX = BM = CM = CY\). Point \(E\) is the foot of the perpendicular from \(B\) to the line \(AC\), and point \(F\) is the foot of the perpendicular from \(C\) to the line \(AB\). Show that \(\angle FNX = \angle YNE\).
