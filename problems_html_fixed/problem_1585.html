Problem 1585 - 2023 IMO, P4 of 6

Difficulty: 6

Let \(x_1, x_2, \dots, x_{2023}\) be pairwise different positive real numbers such that \[a_n = \sqrt{(x_1 + x_2 + \cdots + x_n) \left(\frac{1}{x_1} + \frac{1}{x_2} + \dots + \frac{1}{x_n} \right)}\] is an integer for every \(n=1,2,\dots,2023\). Prove that \(a_{2023} \geq 3034\).
