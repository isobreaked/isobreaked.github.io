Problem 1686 - 2023 Discord SMP Competition P7 of 7

Difficulty: 9

Let \(n\ge3\) be a positive integer. \(A_1,A_2,\dots,A_n\) are (possibly empty) subsets of the set \(\left\{1,2,\dots,\frac{n(n-1)}2\right\}\). It is known that, for any positive integers \(i,j\) satisfying \(1\le i,j\le n\), the set \(A_i\setminus A_j\) has cardinality at most \(n-2\). Prove that the coefficient of the term \[\displaystyle \prod^{\frac{n(n-1)}2}_{i=1} x_i \text{ in } \prod_{1\le i < j\le n}\left(\sum_{k\in A_i}x_k-\sum_{k\in A_j}x_k\right)\] is zero.
