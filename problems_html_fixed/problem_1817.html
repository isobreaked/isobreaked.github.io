Problem 1817 - 2023 Singapore MO, P3 of 5, N

Difficulty: 7

Let \(n \geq 2\) be a positive integer. For a positive integer \(a\), let \(Q_a(x)=x^n+ax\). Let \(p\) be a prime and let \[S_a=\{b \mid 0 \leq b \leq p-1, \exists c \in \mathbf {Z}, Q_a(c) \equiv b \pmod p \}.\] Show that \(\frac{1}{p-1}\sum_{a=1}^{p-1}|S_a|\) is an integer.
