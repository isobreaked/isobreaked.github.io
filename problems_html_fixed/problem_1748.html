Problem 1748 - 2023 China TST Test 3, P5 of 6

Difficulty: 9

Determine whether there exist a sequence of distinct (not necessarily positive) integers \(a_1\), \(a_2\), \(\cdots\) satisfying:

  1. For \(\forall k\in\mathbf N_+\), \(a_{k^2}>0\) and \(a_{k^2+k}<0\);
  2. For \(\forall n\in\mathbf N_+\), \(\left| a_{n+1}-a_n\right|\leqslant 2023\sqrt n\).
