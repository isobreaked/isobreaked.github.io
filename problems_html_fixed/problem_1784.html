Problem 1784 - 2023 DGO Team, P5 of 6, G

Difficulty: 9

Let \(\triangle{ABC}\) be a triangle with points \(X,Y\) lie on segment \(BC\) such that \(AX,AY\) are isogonal in \(\angle{BAC}.\) \(O\) is the circumcenter of \(\triangle{ABC}.\) Lines \(AX,AY\) intersects the circumcircle of \(\triangle{ABC}\) again at points \(X',Y'\) respectively. Points \(N_1,N_2\) are the nine-point centers of \(\triangle{ABY},\triangle{ACX}\) respectively. Line \(BN_1\) intersects line \(CN_2\) at \(W.\) Line \(BY'\) intersects line \(CX'\) at \(R.\) Prove that: \(WR,AO\) are parallel.
