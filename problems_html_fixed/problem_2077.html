Problem 2077 - 2024 USEMO, P3 of 6, G

Difficulty: 9

Let \(ABC\) be a triangle with incenter \(I\). Two distinct points \(P\) and \(Q\) are chosen on the circumcircle of \(ABC\) such that \[\angle API = \angle AQI = 45^\circ.\]Lines \(PQ\) and \(BC\) meet at \(S\). Let \(H\) denote the foot of the altitude from \(A\) to \(BC\). Prove that \(\angle AHI = \angle ISH\).
