Problem 1658 - 2018 Ukraine TST, P9 of 12, G

Difficulty: 8

Let \(AA_1, BB_1, CC_1\) be the heights of triangle \(ABC\) and \(H\) be its orthocenter. Line \(\ell\) parallel to \(AC\), intersects straight lines \(AA_1\) and \(CC_1\) at points \(A_2\) and \(C_2\), respectively. Suppose that point \(B_1\) lies outside the circumscribed circle of triangle \(A_2 HC_2\). Let \(B_1P\) and \(B_1T\) be tangent to of this circle. Prove that points \(A_1, C_1, P\), and \(T\) are cyclic.
