Problem 670 - 2016 IMOSL, N8, N

Difficulty: 9

Find all polynomials \(P(x)\) of odd degree \(d\) and with integer coefficients satisfying the following property: for each positive integer \(n\), there exists \(n\) positive integers \(x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n\) such that \(\frac12 < {P(x_i)}/{P(x_j)} < 2\) and \({P(x_i)}/{P(x_j)}\) is the \(d\)-th power of a rational number for every pair of indices \(i\) and \(j\) with \(1 \leq i, j \leq n\).
