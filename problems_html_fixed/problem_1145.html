Problem 1145 - 2015 EGMO, P6 of 6, G

Difficulty: 7

Let \(H\) be the orthocentre and \(G\) be the centroid of acute-angled triangle \(\triangle ABC\) with \(AB\ne AC\). The line \(AG\) intersects the circumcircle of \(\triangle ABC\) at \(A\) and \(P\). Let \(P'\) be the reflection of \(P\) in the line \(BC\). Prove that \(\angle CAB = 60^\circ\) if and only if \(HG = GP'\).
