Problem 1572 - 2010 Balkan MO, P3 of 4, CG

Difficulty: 7

A strip of width \(w\) is the set of points in the plane which are on, or between, two parallel lines distance \(w\) apart. Let \(S\) be a finite set of \(n\) (\(n\ge3\)) points in the plane, such that any three different points from \(S\) can be covered by a strip of width \(1\). Prove that \(S\) can be covered by a strip of width \(2\).
