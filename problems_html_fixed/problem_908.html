Problem 908 - Folklore

Difficulty: 8

Consider two identical blocks of mass \(1\)kg, initially at \(0^\circ\) and \(100^\circ\) degrees respectively. You are allowed to cut the blocks however you want and touch whatever pieces against each other. Heat transfer happens instantenously without any heat loss. At the end of the process both blocks must be in their original configuration. How hot can you get the first block?
