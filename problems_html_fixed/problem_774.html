Problem 774 - 2021 Japan MO Finals, P5 of 5

Difficulty: 8

Let \(n\) be a positive integer. Find all integers \(k\) among \(1,2,\dots,2n^2\) which satisfy the condition that there is a \(2n\times 2n\) grid of square unit cells such that when \(k\) different cells are painted black while the other cells are painted white, the minimum possible number of \(2\times 2\) squares that contain both black and white cells is \(2n-1\)
