Problem 1852 - 2024 EGMO P5 of 6, NA

Difficulty: 6

Let \(\mathbf{N}\) denote the set of positive integers. Find all functions \(f \colon \mathbf{N} \to \mathbf{N}\) such that the following conditions are true for every pair of positive integers \((x,y)\):

  • \(x\) and \(f(x)\) have the same number of positive divisors.
  • If \(x\) does not divide \(y\) and \(y\) does not divide \(x\) then \[\gcd(f(x),f(y)) > f(\gcd(x,y)).\]
Here \(\gcd(m,n)\) is the largest positive integer that divides both \(m\) and \(n\).
