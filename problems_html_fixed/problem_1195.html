Problem 1195 - 2022 IZHO, P6 of 6

Difficulty: 7

Do there exist two bounded sequences \(a_1,a_2,\dots\) and \(b_1,b_2,\dots\) such that for each positive integers \(n\) and \(m>n\) at least one of the two inequalities \(|a_m-a_n|>\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}, |b_m-b_n|>\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}\) holds?
