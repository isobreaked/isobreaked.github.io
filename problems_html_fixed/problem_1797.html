Problem 1797 - 2023 IMC, P4 of 10 (adapted) , N

Difficulty: 7

Let \(p>2\) be a prime number and let \(k\) be a positive integer. Suppose that the numbers \(a_i=i^k+i\) for \(i=0,1, \ldots,p-1\) leave pairwise distinct remainders apon division by \(p\). What is the set of possible remainders of \(a_2\) upon division by \(p\)?
