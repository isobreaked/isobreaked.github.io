Problem 1399 - 2023 USA TST, P3 of 6

Difficulty: 9

Consider pairs \((f,g)\) of functions from the set of nonnegative integers to itself such that \(f(0) \geq f(1) \geq f(2) \geq \dots \geq f(300) \geq 0\) \(f(0)+f(1)+f(2)+\dots+f(300) \leq 300\) for any 20 nonnegative integers \(n_1, n_2, \dots, n_{20}\), not necessarily distinct, we have\[g(n_1+n_2+\dots+n_{20}) \leq f(n_1)+f(n_2)+\dots+f(n_{20}).\] Determine the maximum possible value of \(g(0)+g(1)+\dots+g(6000)\) over all such pairs of functions.
